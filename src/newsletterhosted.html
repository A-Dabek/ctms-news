CURRENT PROJECT STATUS

At the end of June 2017, the project achieved a major milestone by finalizing the Configuration Specification Phase on target. Our CTMS vendor, BSI, is now implementing the Roche specific configuration within the new solution based on the topics discussed during the four configuration workshops in March-June 2017. Regular demonstrations of these configurations are scheduled with the key users and the project team to ensure the requested specifications are correctly reflected within the system.

WHAT HAVE WE ACHIEVED?

During the Configuration Specification Phase, representatives of each Business Unit (BU) and other Subject Matter Experts reviewed system specifications known as "Use Cases". Use Cases (UCs) are system processes with outlined steps (including owners, a description, defined input and output) defined to achieve a specific goal within a system. In the Configuration Workshops, the team discussed the UCs and aligned on system configurations that will be implemented in the CTMS software to meet our business needs and current processes. The Configuration Specification Phase is now closed.

If you would like to learn more about the discussions, please do not hesitate to reach out to one of your BU Key Users. As a next step we will involve more representatives in the Build to Test Phase to ensure that the Roche system configuration meets business needs.

WHAT'S GOING TO CHANGE FOR YOU?

In Volume 1 of our CTMS Newsletter we addressed the key features of CTMS. Now we would like to provide more details on how the implementation will impact you in addition to some of the main benefits of the system.

General Use of the System

You have the possibility to create a personalized user screen which allows you to see only relevant/selected study information.

A personalized user screen provides customized and quick overview of data most relevant for you

Set-up & Management of Study related Content

Relevant study and site information (e.g. investigator name and study ID) will pre-populate within the study templates available in CTMS.

Pre-populated information reduces the effort of entering site and study related information and therefore reduces the risk of spelling and copy/paste errors

You will always have the right/current and standardized template available in the system

Reporting

With a personal cockpit, you will have an overview (e.g. upcoming, pending and overdue activities) in a central location rather than needing to track in multiple places. Additionally, there will be a reduction of effort in creating and generating reports, including overviews of studies across business areas.

The personal cockpit provides visualization of tasks on a single screen

There will be a better visibility across organizations

Limited effort for you of manual entry and formatting of data for reports and also possibility of customizing reports

Study Planning

A user-friendly workflow allows you to select and add sites to a study. If you choose a site that is not currently available in CTMS, you can add it to the system.

You can benefit from the experience of other studies and sites (including the outcome of the post site assessment)

Managers get an easy overview of the studies covered by a Clinical Validation Plan (CVP)

You will be able to view and filter more information about specific sites of interest

Study Preparation

CTMS will enhance the ease of document tracking and replace the use of old tracking tools (e.g. Excel). For example, Protocol approvals/amendments can be tracked and linked to study milestones. CTMS offers Roche Diagnostics a transparent view on studies ongoing and provides up-to-date information across Business Units (BUs)/ Business Areas (BAs).

The system supports the tracking of items and actions and will provide alerts

The system ensures compliance requirements are met for Firewall studies. Only authorized users will have access to the data

Study Execution

CTMS will provide an online solution with partially automated processes and workflows to enable easier creation and tracking of action items (on both site and study level) plus an automatic, real-time upload to eTMF for certain documents (e.g. monitoring visit reports/letters). The system provides:

Easier tracking of action items

Increased efficiency when creating a monitoring report (supported by the tool)

Increased visibility and standardization of processes and workflows

Visibility of open action items for the next monitoring visit

Study Close-out

All action items must be completed before a study can be closed in CTMS. Additionally, a new formalized request process including a documented reason for change will be used for reopening a closed study. The system enables:

A better overview of open action items and tasks

Full audit trail of changes and reason for change when reopening a closed study
